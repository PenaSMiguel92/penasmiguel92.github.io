Senior Capstone Project: VR CPR

Lead GUI and Game Programmer

More About the Project

Collaborated with six person team, four biomedical engineers (including me) and two electrical engineers, on best practices for developing video games. Advocated for the utilization of the HTC Vive VR System, which included HTV Vive Trackers, Headset, and Base Stations for optimal VR experience. Streamlined development of feedback systems, alignment of models to real-world controllers, and communication with Arduino IDE.

Troubleshot hardware and software problems and resolved them within a few hours of being discovered. Documented troubleshooting steps for future reference. Collaborated with electrical engineers on communication between Unity and Arduino IDE as well as speech recognition software implementation.

Technology Utilized

  • Unity
  • HTC Vive VR System
  • HTC Vive Tracker
  • Leap Hand Tracking
  • Arduino Micro programmed with Arduino IDE
  • Accelerometer attached to glove, connected to Arduino Micro
